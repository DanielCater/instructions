Instructions for a screenshot

For Windows

  1. Press the PrtScn button on your keyboard to capture the entire screen.
  2. Open an image editing program like Paint.
  3. Paste the screenshot by pressing Ctrl + V.
  4. Save the file by clicking "File" > "Save As" and choose your desired format and location.

For Mac

  • Press Shift + Command (⌘) + 3 to capture the entire screen.
  • or Press Shift + Command (⌘) + 4 to capture a selected portion of the screen.
  • or Press Shift + Command (⌘) + 5 to open the screenshot toolbar for more options.
  • The screenshot will be automatically saved to your desktop.

For more detailed instructions, visit Windows 11 Screenshot Instructions or Mac Screenshot Instructions.
